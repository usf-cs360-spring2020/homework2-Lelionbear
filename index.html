Data

Data set was taken from Data SF and shows the Air Traffic Passenger Statistics for the San Francisco international airport.
Data period from July 2005 to September 2019 (as of this date in February 2020)

"This data is made available under the Public Domain Dedication and License v1.0 whose full text can be found at: www.opendatacommons.org/licenses/pddl/1.0/"

Data SF license information

Dataset consists of San Francisco International Airport monthly reports with data on number of passengers and some statistics by airlines. Here is the list of columns:

  1. Activity Period: Number. Shows the data of report in yyyy/mm format.
  2. Operating Airline: Text. Operating airline of a flight.
  3. Operating Airline IATA Code: Text. International Air Transport Association code (location identifier) of operating airline.
  4. Published Airline: Text. Published airline of a flight. (The airline that was supposed to operate the flight).
  5. Published Airline IATA Code:Text. nternational Air Transport Association code (location identifier) of published airline.
  6. GEO Summary: Text. International or Domestic flight.
  7. GEO Region: Text. Geographic region of the flight.
  8. Activity Type Code: Text. Deplaned, enplaned or transit flight code.
  9. Price Category Code: Text. Low fare/other price code.
  10. Terminal: Text. Terminal of arrival, departure or transit.
  11. Boarding Area: Text. Boarding area of the flight.
  12. Passenger Count: Number. Shows the number of passengers for a particular data.

Not all columns were used for my visualizations.
